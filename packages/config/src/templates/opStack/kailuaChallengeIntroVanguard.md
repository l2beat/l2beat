Any actor can submit a ZK fault proof against an existing child proposal via `proveOutputFault` to mark it faulty during the {{maxClockDuration}} challenge period; this blocks the wrong proposal from resolving but does not, by itself, advance the chain. Conflicting sibling proposals (which would survive the tournament and resolve in place of a faulty proposal) can only be submitted by the Vanguard during the `vanguardAdvantage` window.
